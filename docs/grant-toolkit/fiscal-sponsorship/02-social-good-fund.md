## How to submit

Social Good Fund uses an online intake form. Have `00-master-application-answers.md` open and copy/paste.

**Flow:**
1. Go to https://socialgoodfund.org and click "Apply" or "Start a Project."
2. Fill the online form using the master answers.
3. Upload attachments.
4. Submit.

## Tips specific to Social Good Fund

- SGF asks for a **brief project narrative** — use Section 6 from `00-master-application-answers.md`.
- SGF typically wants to know your **fundraising plan for the first 12 months** — use Section 5 from the master doc.
- SGF will ask how you'll **track and report outcomes** — reference https://azgari.org/funders which already describes the logic model and target KPIs.
- They may ask for a **reference or two** who can speak to the founder's capacity. Have two names ready (board members, advisors, or past collaborators).

## After submission

- Acknowledgment usually arrives within 3–5 business days.
- Decision within 2–4 weeks.
- Follow-up after 10 business days if no response: same template as PPF follow-up.
