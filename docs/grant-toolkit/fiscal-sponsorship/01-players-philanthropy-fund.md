# 01 — Players Philanthropy Fund (PPF) — TOP PRIORITY

**Website:** https://playersphilanthropy.com
**Application page:** https://playersphilanthropy.com/apply/ (verify current URL before submitting)
**Fee:** 4% of funds raised (one of the lowest in the industry)
**Typical approval time:** 5–10 business days
**Why they're first:** Lowest fee, fastest approval, straightforward intake process, no geographic restriction, and they routinely accept newer organizations.

5. Submit. Save the confirmation email.

## Tips specific to PPF

- PPF is known for being founder-friendly and fast. Don't overcomplicate the application.
- PPF is a **Model C** fiscal sponsor — they host your project as a restricted fund. You operate under your own brand (Azgari Foundation), and PPF holds the funds and issues donor receipts under their EIN until your 501(c)(3) determination arrives.
- PPF will likely ask for an **anticipated annual revenue** number — say $250,000 (matching the master doc budget).
- When PPF approves you, they'll assign a **project manager** as your point of contact and provide a **donation URL** that you can put on azgari.org. Save that URL — we'll wire it into the site next.

## After submission

- Expect acknowledgment within 1–2 business days.
- Expect a decision within 5–10 business days.
- If you don't hear back in 7 days, send a brief follow-up: *"Just checking in on the fiscal-sponsorship application I submitted on [date] for Azgari Foundation — happy to answer any additional questions."*
